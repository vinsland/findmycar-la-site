Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: June 1, 2026

FindMyCar LA collects only the information needed to review your car sourcing request and contact you about next steps.

Information We Collect

When you submit the lead form, we may collect your budget, preferred make and model, year range, mileage preference, phone number, and any details you choose to provide.

How We Use Your Information

  • To review your vehicle preferences and budget.
  • To contact you about your request.
  • To estimate a realistic auction buying range.
  • To provide car sourcing and inspection-related support.

Form Processing

Our form submissions are processed through Formspree. Formspree may receive and store form data so the inquiry can be delivered to us.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share details only when needed to provide the requested service, comply with legal requirements, or work with service providers that help operate the website and form.

Data Retention

We keep inquiry information only as long as reasonably needed to respond to you, provide service, maintain business records, or comply with applicable obligations.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update or delete your inquiry information by contacting us through the same phone or communication channel used for your request.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date above shows when the latest version was posted.
